Codes

In this section, the wiki will talk about the rebuilt codes in Site-13-V2 repository. Old codes are too messy, scary and hard to maintain.

Design Principal

Codes in Site-13 should follow principle below:

  • Use less MonoBehaviour messages.
  • Put codes in Site13Kernel and Site13Kernel.UnityEngine as much as possible to reduce comile time.
  • Use AggressiveInlining option on most of methods.

Code Structure

Codes of Site-13 are splited into two parts: i) Codes in Tools/CommonTools/; ii) Codes directly stored in Site-13-V2/Assets/SCPAssets/Codes.

The most important part of codes are:

  • Site13Kernel
  • Site13Kernel.UnityEngine
  • Site-13-V2/Assets/SCPAssets/Codes
